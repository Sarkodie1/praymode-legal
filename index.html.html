Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 30, 2026

Welcome to PrayMode. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with our core services, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and authentication credentials.
  • App Activity & Usage Data: To enable the "Focus Mode" (App Locker) functionality, our app requests the Android "Usage Access" permission. We collect real-time data about which applications are currently open on your device.
  • Prayer Activity: We store your prayer streaks, schedules, and preferences to provide you with a personalized experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To Enforce Focus Mode: The "Usage Access" permission is strictly used locally on your device to detect when you open a restricted application. This allows PrayMode to trigger the lock screen and encourage your daily prayer habits. We do not transmit your overall app usage history to our servers.
  • To Manage Your Account: Your email is used to securely authenticate your identity and sync your prayer progress across devices.
  • To Improve the App: To analyze crash reports and improve app stability.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We only share your data with trusted third-party service providers (such as Supabase for database and authentication hosting) strictly for the purpose of operating the application. All data transmitted to our servers is encrypted in transit.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your personal data only for as long as your account is active. You have the right to request the deletion of your account and associated data at any time. You can initiate a deletion request from within the app settings or by contacting us directly.

5. Children's Privacy

Our application is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
